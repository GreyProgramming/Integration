Be Whoever You Want To Be.

This is by no means an accident - in the recently released

Waterdeep: Dungeon of the Mad Mage

, there is a questline where you return an NPC to her wife, an obvious LGBT storyline.

Further, roleplaying games allow you to explore ideas you have about yourself in a safe fantasy environment, so you can see how various aspects of the character interract and how you feel about them.
